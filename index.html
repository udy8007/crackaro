Safety Guidelines

Celebrate responsibly

Please follow these standard precautions for a safe and enjoyable festival.

Adult Supervision

Children must use crackers only under adult supervision in open spaces.

Water Ready

Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby while lighting fireworks.

No Enclosed Areas

Never light crackers indoors, near vehicles, or under roofs.

First Aid Ready

Keep a basic first-aid kit available during celebrations.
